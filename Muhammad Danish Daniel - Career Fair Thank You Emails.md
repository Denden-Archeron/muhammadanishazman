## Quick Answer: Can You Send Thank You Emails to Hiring Emails?

**YES.** Hiring/recruitment emails (e.g., careers@company.com, recruitment@company.com) are specifically set up to receive candidate communications. Sending a thank you follow-up to these addresses is:

1. **Professional** — It shows you are serious about the opportunity
2. **Expected** — Many recruiters wait for follow-ups to gauge genuine interest
3. **Differentiating** — Most career fair attendees never follow up. You will stand out
4. **Standard practice** — This is how candidates without personal recruiter emails communicate

The recruitment team reads these inboxes. Your email will be seen.
